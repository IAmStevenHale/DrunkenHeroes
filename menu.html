Drunken Heroes
What happens when Heroes Party?


Rules

Whoever can chug the fastest starts. If two or more people believe that their superpower is chugging, than a chug-off will be the decider.

Draw a card and read it aloud (unless it says otherwise). Follow the instructions on the card and try to survive.
Some cards will be marked "SECRET" - do not read these ones out loud.
Some cards can be held until later on - try and remember!
There are rare cards that allow you to gain permenant superpowers - but at what cost?!

The Winner is the last Hero standing... Seriously though, the winner is the person that can prove that they are the ultimate Drunken Hero by not passing out.

Remember the aim is to have fun... who am I kidding, the aim is to win.
Crush your enemies and your friends!
